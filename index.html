My Story

After years of working in the Insurance sector, I was able to retire and pursue my love of photography. I love many aspects of photography, but in the last several years, my friends turned me on to horse-racing events. I now travel the U.S. photographing racing events and couldn't be happier. My work is published in magazines and other media. I love traveling and searching for the unicorn.
